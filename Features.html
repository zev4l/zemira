Features

  1. Multiple card-packs available:

    Through the store, the player can obtain new and different card-packs that alter the game's style.

  2. Various game modes:

    The game supports several game modes: playing with card pairs, playing with card trios and race against the clock mode.

  3. Multiplayer:

    This game can be played individually or with friends in local multiplayer.>

  4. Progress & Leaderboard:

    If an account is created, the player's progress will be saved. This includes all of the stats, which will also be compared with other players' stats on the leaderboard.
