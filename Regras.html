Game rules!

    Single-player:

  1. Each player chooses two cards and if the cards are the same, he receives a point and the cards are removed from the game table.
  2. If the cards the player chose are different, they return to their original position.
  3. The game ends when all pairs of cards are discovered.

    Multiplayer:

  1. The first player to play is chosen randomly.
  2. Each player plays only once, even if he hits a pair of cards.
  3. If the player hits a pair of cards he receives a point.
  4. The game ends when all pairs of cards are discovered.
  5. The player with the most points wins.
  6. If all players have the same number of points, a tie is declared.
