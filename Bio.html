Bio

The three creators of the website and this game, attend the Faculty of Sciences of Lisbon.

They are currently in the first year of the Information Technology Degree course, and this work resulted from the project proposal by the professors of the Introduction to Web Technologies course.

The three authors are in this course due to their passion for technology and insatiable curiosity for more, especially in the technology department.

This project allowed them to learn HTML5, JavaScript and CSS, being the three important tools for the world of programming, namely in the area of ​​web page development.
